My Favorite Movies

Dazed and Confused

Dazed and Confused, which came out in 1993, is a movie about high school seniors on summer vacation in the 70's. It features a cast including Matthew McConaughey, Ben Affleck, Jason London, and others.

Catch Me If You Can

Catch Me If You Can is a movie about Frank Abagnale Jr, a famous con artist who successfully conned many people over in the 1980s. The movie stars a young Leonardo DiCaprio and Tom Hanks, and is widely regarded as a classic thriller. The movie was based on a book written by Frank Abagnale himself, however a recent study found that most of his stories were greatly exaggerated.
